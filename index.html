StudentSpot was created to give students help with planning, scheduling, etc. Only other students can really tell you what different classes are like.

After realizing this, we recruited a team of students to create a place that student could learn from their peers about classes. After conducting surveys of multiple students and teachers, StudentSpot was released to the school.
